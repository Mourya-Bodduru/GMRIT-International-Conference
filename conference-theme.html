Conference Theme

First Topic | Environment and Renewable Energies

One of the most important goals of sustainable development is to provide clean and affordable energy. This shift in providing an alternative to fossil energy sources will improve the environmental reality and provide clean and renewable energy that is not affected by political and economic shocks. The focus of the topic is:

  • Renewable and clean energy technologies
  • Energy Management and Efficiency
  • Electronic sensors and remote sensing systems
  • Electric Vehicle Technologies
  • Power Electronics and Control
  • Robotics and Embedded systems


Second Topic | Artificial intelligence,Machine learning, and Quantum applications

  • Quantum Computing
  • Artificial Neural Networks, Deep Learning & Natural Language Processing
  • Image processing and Pattern recognition
  • Computer Networks & Data Communications
  • ML, DL, &NLP in security
  • ML, DL, &NLP in medical image processing
  • Hyper Spectral Image processing using DL
  • AI in Big data infrastructure


Third Topic | Science, Engineering & Technology

  • Nanotechnology and Smart Materials
  • Bioinformatics
  • Polymers and Plastics
  • Instrumentation and Control Engineering
  • Environmental Remediations
  • Advanced manufacturing and its Applications
  • Mechanical Behaviour of Materials
  • Materials Design and processing for Emerging Technologies
  • Modelling and Simulation in Design, Thermal and Manufacturing Technologies


We invite all original unpublished research articles based on the theme but not limited to.


All accepted manuscripts will be published in Scopus indexed conference proceedings.
